№ 01 / 07 · Of long-duration custodianship

Inertum.
A protocol for long-duration value preservation.

Engineered for institutional custody. Audited by independent laboratories. Documented to museum standard.1

Each capsule combines a polymer-foil laminate, an inert atmosphere, an iron-based scavenger, and a passive optical sentinel. The protocol is designed to preserve value across fifty years without intervention.2

This document is the dossier. Specifications appear in §II. The catalog of custody formats is presented in §III.
