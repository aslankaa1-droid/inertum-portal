I. Provenance

A protocol, not a product.

«To preserve is to refuse the verdict of time. INERTUM does not arrest decay — it documents its absence.»

Each capsule is engineered as a single archival event. A polymer-foil laminate, certified to ГОСТ 9.014 and DEF STAN 81-147. An inert atmosphere of pure nitrogen or argon, sealed below 0.5% residual oxygen. An iron-based scavenger as silent witness.

The optical sentinel — a passive luminescent spot — reads the capsule from outside. No battery. No transmitter. Only light, returned through a transparent window in the laminate.

Each unit bears its own custody record. Each record is signed.
