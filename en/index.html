§01 · PROBLEM

Premium assets lose value to oxidation and corrosion

Existing solutions last 5–10 years, not 50. Between them — a $50B+ blind spot in collector cars, art, pharma, and strategic reserves.

Collector cars

Metal corrosion, rubber and leather oxidation. Up to 5–10% annual value loss in seasonal storage without proper packaging.

Museum artifacts

Metal oxidation, textile fading, biological degradation. Up to 15% of collections inaccessible to the public due to damage.

Pharma & reserves

Up to 15% of inventory written off due to storage breaches. Strategic drug reserves — billions in losses every year.

Premium segment

Whisky, wine, watches, jewelry — oxidation of active components and visual deterioration in ambient air.

VCI films don't scale

Cortec and Zerust volatile inhibitors run for 5–10 years, not 50. Not suitable for electronics, pharma, or long-duration use.

Mil-spec is for parts

MIL-STD-2073, DEF STAN 81-147 cover individual components, not whole items, with no integrated leak monitoring.

§02 · TECHNOLOGY

Five layers of protection in a single capsule

Hybrid architecture: barrier laminate, inert atmosphere, chemical scavenger, passive optical sensing, and self-diagnostic leak detection. No batteries, no radio signal.

Layer 01 · Barrier
PET / Al-foil / LLDPE · OTR ≤ 0.05 cm³/m²·day · MIL-PRF-131K · DEF STAN 81-147 · ISO 11607
Layer 02 · Inert atmosphere
N₂ or Ar · O₂ ≤ 0.5% · dew point ≤ −45 °C · purge per ISO 14644
Layer 03 · O₂ scavenger
Mitsubishi Ageless Z / RP System / Pharmakeep · iron-based and organic — chemically dual-redundant with the gas purge
Layer 04 · Passive sensing
Optical spot · 5–7 years autonomous · contactless readout through transparent window · zero battery
Layer 05 · Self-diagnostics
Correlation of ΔP ↔ Δ[O₂] ↔ ΔT · auto-alert at leak onset, before any visible damage
